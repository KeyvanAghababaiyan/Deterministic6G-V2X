Deterministic 6G-V2X Systems: A New Paradigm for Reliable V2X Networks

A project on deterministic communication–computing–control for connected and automated vehicles across the local–edge–cloud continuum.

Deterministic6G-V2X explores how future 6G networks can provide bounded end-to-end latency and reliability for safety-critical V2X services, integrating communication, computing, and control into a single deterministic architecture.

Overview

The project combines queueing-theoretic analysis, network simulation, and experimental validation to design and evaluate deterministic mechanisms for 6G-enabled cooperative driving. Work packages cover theoretical foundations, system design, implementation, and dissemination.
